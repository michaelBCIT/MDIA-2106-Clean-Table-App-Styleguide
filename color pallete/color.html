Color Palette

“SEAWEED” is the main color of the “Clean Table”. It is a green-cyan. The shade was chosen because of the popularity within the target audience. It represents restful, creativity, protection, and joy.

“MANGO” is one of the accent colors that will be used in “Clean Table”. This shade was chosen as it signifies the meaning of happiness, optimisim, and creativity which are all important when it comes to users using out app.

“SALMON” is a shade of geraldine; medium light shade of red. This shade was chosen because it has a good contrast between the shade “SEAWEED”. It represents playfulness, kindess, passionate, and caring.

“BLACKBERRY” will be used for most of the text in “Clean Table”. This color was chosen as it has a good contrast bewteen the background colors and the text.. Good contrast is important for easy readilbity and usabilty.

"COCONUT" is a shade that will be used for white space to keep the application from appearing too crowded. Using “snow” gives a cleaner look throughout the application and will allow users to focus more on the content.
