Color Palette

“MANGO” is one of the accent colors that will be used in “Clean Table”. This shade was chosen as it signifies the meaning of happiness, optimisim, and creativity which are all important when it comes to users using out app.

"COCONUT"is a shade that will be used for white space to keep the application from appearing too crowded. Using “snow” gives a cleaner look throughout the application and will allow users to focus more on the content.
